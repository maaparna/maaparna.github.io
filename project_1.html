The IMDB movies and ratings

This page gives you view of the IMDB ratings of the movies from the year 2006 to 2016
The chart on the left consolidates the rating by Genre for the selected year.
When a genre is selected a new chart is created below that lists the movies and its ratings below.
In the bottom chart when a movie is selected a tooltip box with further details about the movie will be displayed.
Also, note that the same process would apply to any year selected in the dropdown list provided below the title.
